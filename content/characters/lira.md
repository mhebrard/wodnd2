# Lira the Rogue

Lira grew up on the streets of Eldoria, learning to survive by her wits and agility. She is quick with a blade and even quicker with a clever remark.

## Attributes
- Strength: 8
- Dexterity: 16
- Constitution: 12
- Intelligence: 13
- Wisdom: 10
- Charisma: 14

## Skills
- Stealth: +5
- Acrobatics: +4
- Sleight of Hand: +4
- Perception: +2

## Equipment
- Dagger
- Leather Armor
- Thieves' Tools

## Story
Lira's journey began when she overheard a plot to kidnap the prince. Now, she seeks allies to thwart the conspiracy and prove her worth to the city she calls home.
