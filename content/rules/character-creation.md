# Character Creation

## Step-by-Step

- **Concept**: Define the concept of your character: few words.
- **Race**: Choose your race. Notice the Attributes and Merits that correspond.
- **Attributes**: Prioritize the columns. In addition to the initial Dots (see Races), you can spend 5 / 4 / 3 Dots by column. The fifth Dot cost two Dots.
- **Skills**: Prioritize the categories. You can spend 11 / 7 / 4 Dots by category. The fifth Dot cost two Dots.
- **Specialties**: You get three Skills Specialties. Expertise area that adds 1 dice to your pool. You can have multiple specialties on the same Skill.
- **Advantages**: Fill the traits derived from your Attributes. 
  - **Size**: See Races
  - **Speed**: Strength + Dexterity + Size
  - **Defense**: The lowest of Dexterity or Wits
  - **Initiative**: Dexterity + Composure
- **Health**: Stamina + Size note a malus of -1/-2/-3 for the last points
- **Willpower**: Resolve + Composure
- **Merits**: See Races for appropriate Merits that represent character enhancements and background elements. The fifth Dot cost two Dots.
- **Magic**: Mastery starts at 1 dot, additional dots can be purchased for 3 Merits dots.
- **Final**: Fill the character Name, and list the Equipment she carries

## Races

- **Human**: Size 6. All Attributes start at 1 dot. Merits: +8 dots.
- **Elf**: Size 6, All Attributes start at 1 dot but Dexterity at 2 dots and Manipulation at 0 dot. Merits: Natural Immunity 1 dot, Toxins Resistance 2 dots, Weather Immunity 1 dot, + 4 dots.
- **Hobbit**: Size 3, All Attributes start at 1 dot but Manipulation at 2 dots and Strength at 0 dot. Merits: Indomitable 2 dots, Small Framed 2 dots, + 4 dots.
- **Dwarf**: Size 4, All Attributes start at 1 dot but Stamina at 2 dots and Composure at 0 dot. Merits: Iron Stamina 3 dots, Magi Immunity 1 dot, + 4 dots.
