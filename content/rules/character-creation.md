# Character Creation

## Step 1: Concept
Choose a concept for your character. Who are they? What do they do?
- **Race**: Choose from standard fantasy races (Human, Elf, Dwarf, etc.).
- **Class**: Choose a class (Fighter, Mage, Rogue, etc.) which determines your starting abilities.

## Step 2: Attributes
Prioritize your three categories: Mental, Physical, Social.
- **Primary**: 5 dots to distribute.
- **Secondary**: 4 dots to distribute.
- **Tertiary**: 3 dots to distribute.
*Note: All Attributes start at 1 dot automatically.*

## Step 3: Skills
Prioritize your three categories: Mental, Physical, Social.
- **Primary**: 11 dots.
- **Secondary**: 7 dots.
- **Tertiary**: 4 dots.

## Step 4: Merits & Advantages
Select Merits that define your character's special capabilities.

## Step 5: Derived Traits
Calculate Health, Willpower, Size, Speed, Defense, and Initiative.
