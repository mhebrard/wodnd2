# Core Rules

## The Dice Pool
The core mechanic of WoDnD2 is the **Dice Pool**. Whenever a character attempts an action with an uncertain outcome, the player rolls a number of 10-sided dice (d10) equal to their **Attribute + Skill**.

- **Success**: Any die showing an **8, 9, or 10** is a success.
- **Failure**: If no dice show 8+, the action fails.
- **Exceptional Success**: If you roll **5 or more successes**, the action succeeds with extra benefits.

## Attributes
Attributes represent a character's raw potential. They are rated from 1 to 5 dots.

### Mental
- **Intelligence**: Raw computing power and memory.
- **Wits**: Thinking on your feet and reaction time.
- **Resolve**: Focus and determination.

### Physical
- **Strength**: Physical power.
- **Dexterity**: Agility and coordination.
- **Stamina**: Toughness and health.

### Social
- **Presence**: Charisma and force of personality.
- **Manipulation**: Subtlety and ability to influence others.
- **Composure**: Emotional control and calm.
