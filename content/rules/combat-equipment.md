# Combat

## Initiative
At the start of combat, everyone rolls **1d10 + Initiative Modifier**. Highest goes first.

## The Combat Turn
1. **Move**: You can move up to your Speed in meters.
2. **Action**: Attack, cast a spell, or use a skill.

## Attacking
- **Melee**: Strength + Weaponry - Defense.
- **Ranged**: Dexterity + Firearms/Archery - Cover.
- **Unarmed**: Strength + Brawl - Defense.

## Damage
Weapons add bonus dice to your attack pool.
- **Bashing**: Blunt trauma. Heals quickly.
- **Lethal**: Cuts and gunshots. Heals slowly.
- **Aggravated**: Supernatural damage. Very hard to heal.

# Equipment

## Weapons
- **Dagger**: +1L, Size 1
- **Sword**: +3L, Size 2
- **Greatsword**: +4L, Size 3, Two-handed

## Armor
Armor reduces the damage taken from successful attacks.
- **Leather**: 1/0
- **Chainmail**: 2/1
- **Plate**: 3/2
